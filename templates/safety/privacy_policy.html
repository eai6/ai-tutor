Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

AI Tutor ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially students and minors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • School/institution affiliation
  • Date of birth (for age verification)

2.2 Learning Data

During tutoring sessions, we collect:

  • Questions you ask and answers you provide
  • Session duration and progress
  • Learning outcomes and mastery levels

2.3 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and browser information
  • IP address (anonymized after 30 days)
  • Usage patterns and timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized AI tutoring services
  • Track your learning progress
  • Improve our educational content and AI models
  • Ensure platform safety and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Child Protection (COPPA Compliance)

We take special care to protect children under 13:

  • Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • For users under 16, we require parental consent for data processing
  • AI interactions are filtered for age-appropriate content
  • We never share children's data with third parties for marketing

5. Data Protection Measures

We protect your data through:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Content safety filters on all AI interactions
  • Automatic PII detection and redaction
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Regular security audits

6. Data Retention

  • Learning session data: 90 days
  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Anonymized analytics: Up to 2 years
  • Security logs: 1 year

7. Your Rights (GDPR/FERPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access - View all data we hold about you
  • Portability - Download your data in a standard format
  • Correction - Update inaccurate information
  • Erasure - Delete your data and account
  • Restriction - Limit how we process your data
  • Withdraw Consent - Change your consent preferences anytime

Exercise these rights in your Privacy Dashboard.

8. AI and Automated Processing

Our AI tutor:

  • Uses your responses to provide personalized feedback
  • Does not make decisions that significantly affect you without human oversight
  • Is monitored for bias and inappropriate content
  • Never shares your personal conversations with other users

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AI language models (for tutoring) - No personal data shared
  • Cloud hosting (for data storage) - Data encrypted

We do not sell your data to third parties.

10. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data requests:

  • Email: privacy@aitutor.edu
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@aitutor.edu

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.
